Description of Target Audience

Who: Travelers in Southern Idaho, Residents of these three towns, Bear Lake swimmers

Age: 16-99

Technology: Any size screen with access to the internet

Income: $25,000+

Motivation: Receive real time information on the weather and town news

Five Scenarios

  1. What is the weather like in my town right now?
  2. Are there any exciting events happening in my town soon?
  3. How does Stormy Horizons collect all this data?
