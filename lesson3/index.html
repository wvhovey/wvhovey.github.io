Site Name

The site name for my Weather Site Project is: Stormy Horizons.

Site purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide weather updates for 3 towns in Idaho: Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Each page on this site will have a header with a logo, site title, and site motto. Each of these pages will be easily accessed through a navigation bar directly under the header. Each page will have a footer that contains contact information, a location map, copyright information, author, terms of use link and the current date. The 6 main pages in this site will be: Home, Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Storm Center, and Gallery. The home page will have a weather story, image, and information for each of the three towns being covered. The three pages that are titled after the town names will go into more depth on the weather and town information respective to the town. The images shown will display the current state of the weather in each town. All of this will be created responsively for easy viewing on any device the site is viewed on.
