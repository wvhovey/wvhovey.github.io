Food Truck Spotlight!

The Chicken and Rice Guys
Interested in something filling and savory to get you through rush hour? Look no further than the Chicken and Rice Guys. Offering an array of falafel options with different types of meat and authentic Greek sauces. You can try them today at the square by South Station and outside Tufts Medical Center!

Cookie Monstah
Maybe looking for something to squash your sweet tooth? Cookie Monstah may be the truck for you! They offer a variety of homemade ice cream in cups, waffle cones, and most famously in between two delicious cookies! You can find them every day from 12pm - 6pm at the Green Carousel near Faneuil Hall.

The Bacon Truck
BACON! Need we say more? Get anything and everything bacon related for a hearty, tasty breakfast! These low prices combined with great food are a win for your taste buds and a win for your wallet. Look for them at Brigham and Women's Hospital and near 71 Shattuck Street from 11am - 2pm.

Chamber Purpose and Goals

This chamber began because the owners attempted to follow their dreams to start a food truck while they were in their early 20's. They had no support, and they failed many times until, by pure chance, they succeeded. It is the goal of this chamber to prevent that from happening to anyone who is attempting to run a food truck in the greater Boston area. We offer financial assistance from the high taxes of Massachusetts, legal advice on how to begin the process of food trucking, regular luncheons, free advertisement on social media platforms with access to our advertisement department, and so much more. Every food truck in Boston should be part of Boston's Booming Businesses and take advantage of the help found within this chamber.
