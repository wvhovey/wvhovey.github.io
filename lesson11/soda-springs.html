Sodalicious, not so delicious for your wallet

COVID brought many changes, including a large gain in weight for many people stuck in quarantine. This is partly due to an increase in soda intake and sedentary lifestyles in Soda Springs. With major soda companies unable to keep up with the crazy demand of soda, prices are skyrocketing to $7 per 2 liter and even more if you want the luxury of a can. It might be a good time to switch to water or a healthy alternative, because if you aren't hurting after drinking that much soda, your wallet sure will be.
