Spuds are back on the menu boys

Mash em, boil em, stick em in a stew! Potato prices dropping finally after COVID-19 brought bags to almost $11 in Preston. For the first time in a year, a 5 pound bag now costs around $3 at your local grocery store. Potato crops are booming now more than ever, after an increase in demand led to farmers increasing their crop count. Combined with a good year, potatoes are likely to drop to $0.10 per spud! I guess we'll be eating a lot of potato soups, stews, and everyone will be eating their body weight in mashed potatoes at Thanksgiving.
