New Rise to Tech Companies

With the sudden rise to tech companies in the Preston Area, housing developments, restaurants and businesses are on the rise. The increase of jobs has prompted massive migration which has increased the population of Preston by 15% in just the last year! With more demand for food and other businesses, restaurants have grown in number, size, and variety. As the dynamic of Preston only seems to be getting better, we only have one thing to say, "The more the merrier!"
